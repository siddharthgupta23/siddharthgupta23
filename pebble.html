Pebble Beach Dining

qualia's beachside contemporary dining restaurant. A celebration of
our country’s diversity of produce, culture and cuisine.

The most exclusive of the Hamilton Island restaurants for lunchtime dining, Pebble Beach
highlights qualia’s island setting as shadows lengthen into the afternoon and chilled cocktails
are served by the infinity-edge pool.

In the evening, Pebble Beach is transformed into qualia’s signature dining experience. Food is
contemporary Australian cuisine featuring seasonal, sustainable and ethical produce sourced
from the like minded farmers, producers and artisans around the country. Dishes are produce
and flavour focused. Expect an Asian lean to menus coupled with European technique and
aesthetic.

Each elegantly presented dish is a work of art, and Pebble Beach fine dining is complemented
by premium Australian and imported wines from the restaurant’s superb sommelier-compiled
wine list.

Our Executive Chef, Mark Jensen, and his team have refined the Pebble Beach menu to
showcase the marvellous produce available locally from the Whitsunday region, vegetables
grown on island and those found throughout regional Australia.

Offering Pebble Beach’s signature Tasting Menu which features six courses. It is a culinary
celebration worthy of any special occasion - one that can be exquisitely paired with matching
Australia and international wines.

Creating the ultimate expression of elegance, balance of flavours and presentation – it is the
signature dining experience on Hamilton Island.
