Hamilton Island Golf Club

Relax the Mind, Body and Soul

Lull your senses and relax mind, body and soul on the sun-drenched private sundeck of your
luxury qualia pavilion. Comfortable sunbeds are cooled by gentle breezes, and time drifts by to
the calming palette of blue sea and sky.

Complimentary Naturopathy Information Sessions

Held weekly, please check with Guest Services for session times.
Take the opportunity to listen and learn from our highly qualified Naturopath at Spa qualia. This
is a complimentary and interactive health seminar focussing on topics such as; Detoxing, Healthy
aging, Super foods, Acid/alkaline balance, Iridology and the many health conditions that are
prevalent in today’s society. The spa welcomes all guests to attend this very interesting and
informative session, assisting in prioritising your health and well being this holiday.

Irresistible Swimming Pools

qualia’s two irresistible infinity-edge swimming pools frame the Whitsundays beauty of the Long
Pavilion and Pebble Beach restaurants. Relax into laidback beach-club mode by ordering poolside
drinks from the Long Pavilion wine bar, or a lazy lunch served by the pool at Pebble Beach.

Tranquil Library

Find quiet time in qualia’s tranquil library, where you'll find a collection of carefully
selected books.

Luxurious Day Spa

And for sheer pampering pleasure, indulge in the deep sensory experience of a chardonnay vinotherapy
massage or cooling mist rain shower at Spa qualia. Awaken your senses with qualia yoga and meditation,
and feel invigorated and uplifted with dreaming rituals inspired by indigenous Australian therapies.

Lunch Special

$69 per person, includes return Island Link transfers from Hamilton Island to Dent Island, a two-
course a la carte lunch at the Clubhouse, and a complimentary tour of the Hamilton Island Golf
Course.
