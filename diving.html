Diving and Snorkelling in the Whitsundays

Glide above the fringing coral reef surrounding Hamilton Island, or explore the exquisite white
sands of Whitehaven Beach where you can:

  • let your feet sink into the sparkling white silica sand;
  • ake a dip in the warm tropical waters;
  • explore Hill Inlet and discover the rainbow swirls of turquoise, blue and green created by
    the tide shifting the sand and water.
