Luxury Accommodation: Leeward Pavilion

Relax and unwind in a sun-filled Leeward Pavilion, creatively designed to celebrate the
Whitsundays’ legendary sunsets.

Southwest-facing, the freestanding Leeward Pavilions provide Hamilton Island luxury
accommodation in Australian tropical island style.

qualia is exclusively for guests ages 16 or over.

qualia offers a Leeward Pavilion that has been configured for easy wheelchair access, as well
as providing all the standard Leeward Pavilion facilities. Key features include private outdoor
garden area with water views, wide internal door spaces, fully accessible bathroom, including
appropriate hand railings and a suitably lowered duel hand basin. There are no steps
throughout the pavilion (balcony and courtyard included) and all fittings have been suitably
height adjusted.
