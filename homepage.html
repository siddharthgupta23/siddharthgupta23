Welcome to Qualia

A world-class luxury resort in the Whitsundays. Qualia is a private world of sensory perfection on Hamilton Island.

Qualia (pronounced kwah-lee-ah) in Latin means "a collection of deeper sensory experiences". Qualia is situated on the secluded northern-most tip of Hamilton Island surrounded by all the splendor of the Great Barrier Reef. With a relaxed aspect and mesmerizing calmness, time seems to stretch forever. Qualia is exclusively for guests aged 16 or over.
